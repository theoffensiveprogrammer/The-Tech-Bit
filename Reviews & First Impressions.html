iPad 3 first impressions:

This is my first impressions of the iPad 3rd generation.

  1. The new iPad looks similar to the last generation iPad.
  2. The iPad has a very beautiful screen that makes it a pleasure to use
  3. Upon turning on the device I almost instantly notice that the device seems quicker
  4. Finally the new camera does not dissapoint although using it on a daily basis is still out of the question

So there is my initial impressions of the new iPad. There will be a full review as soon as I feel I have spent enough time with the device to make a judgement.
